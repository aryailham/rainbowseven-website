Breacher

Specialty :

Capable of breach the non steel window and door in a seconds using her breacher device, and also capable of destroy the protector's defense item in a range

Biography :

Lilia Flure are known as Breacher. Her nickname come from her specialty which is breach any non steel items from a range. She is very talented woman who can predict a good time to strike and create a shock moment for the enemies. She was born in Texas, United States. Her role model is her father who enlisted in the Federal Bureau of Investigation. She really admired her father when she was a kid. To follow her father's path, she also enlisted in the Federal Bureau of Investigation.

Breaker

Specialty :

Capable of break any non steel items using his titanium hammer from a close range.

Biography :

Born in London, England from a father who is a businessman and a mother who is a doctor. Given the name Samuel Evan, which is known as Breaker. When Evan was a kid, he always want to enter the army, but his father don't allow it and ask him to be a businessman too. But Evan did not give up and always persuaded his father, finally his father agreed to Evan's dream to enter the army. When Evan was old enough to enter the army, Evan succeed to enter the army and over time, he later entered SAS. With a hammer that his father bought as a gift of his success, he always carried out his mission with the hammer from his father.

Shield

Specialty :

Capable of providing a full body shield that can cover his body and his teammates, and also can block any projectiles.

Biography :

Paul René born in Paris, France. Paul is a very talented GIGN's police. He is very skilled in a missions like counter-terrorism, and surveillance of national threats. He was trained when he was still a kid by his grandfather who was a senior in the anti-terrorist police department. But a tragic incident occurred. While rescuing hostages, the terrorists found out and then ambushed Paul and his team in which Paul was able to save the hostage, but his team members died as a result of the ambush. Paul was traumatized by not being able to save his team members. From then on, Paul René then turned into a protector for his new team by being provided with a large shield in the hope that the incident would not happen again.

Droner

Specialty :

Capable of using and control drones that can deal damage to the enemies.

Biography :

Chloé Ramielle is a GIGN's police who likes to use drones to carry out her mission. Chloé Ramielle was born in Bordeaux, France. Ramielle since she was a child was quiet and did not like to interact with other people. On her 12th birthday, his father bought her a drone. Ramielle was very pleased with the gift and always played the drone from her father. His father saw that Ramielle was very fond of drones and always bought her the newest drone on her birthday. Unexpectedly, Ramielle became an expert in controlling and designing a drone which later greatly helped her in carrying out her mission as a police at GIGN. From there, Ramielle was known as Droner.
